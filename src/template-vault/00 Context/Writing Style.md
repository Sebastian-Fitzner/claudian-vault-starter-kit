# Writing Style and Tone

## Core Tone

Casual, direct, to the point. No fluff. Precision over verbosity.

## Address

Du (informal you). Keep it approachable.

## Rules

- Short and precise
- Direct communication
- No unnecessary jargon
- Development content in English
- Strategy content can be in German

## Avoid

- Overexplaining
- Corporate speak
- Unnecessary formality
